The People’s Law School is a non-profit charitable society providing public legal education and information to British Columbians since 1972.
Our mandate is to facilitate and promote the development and distribution of public legal education and information for people in BC.
Our efforts and results are focused on a BC where everyone is legally literate and has the capacity to exercise their legal rights and responsibilities.
